About Dump

Dump is a site for users to upload their own articles about What's going on around them. They can tag it with a school or local area which tells us what users to share it with. We have our own "School Districts" where content uploaded is specifically about what's going on around their school where Students can share their own opinions on what's actually happening, with no censorship apart from where it doesnt comply with our Community Guidelines.

About School Districts

School Districts are where where content uploaded is specifically about what's going on around their school. It is a place where Students can share their own opinions on what's actually happening, with no censorship apart from where it doesnt comply with our Community Guidelines.

How they work

Schools that have been setup with School Districts have registered their email with us. When you sign up for a account you will enter in your school email, and once verified, will connect you too your school's Dump.
